About

I am a business major student that has landed himself in the information technology space. I have a passion for solving problems and what better way to do this through technology. Finding viable business solutions using state of the art software is my passion.

I grew up in a small rural town in Limpopo, South Africa. With little to no access to major technological advancements, the passion has been kept alive in various ways, from helping friends with problems they are experiencing on their phones to fiddling with the decoder settings until the TV doesn't work anymore. I have always been curious and intrigued as to how these gadgets were built and how they work.

I also have an interest in giving back to those who are less fortunate than us. I like to do this by working with non-profit organisations. It is always satisfying to see someone recieve any kind of help that leaves them in a better place than they previously were.
